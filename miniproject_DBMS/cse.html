The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2002 and since its inception, 
the department has progressed with a vision and a strong commitment in developing competent Engineers with
uncompromising standards of excellence in training, both through class room sessions and hands on sessions. 
The Department is equipped with competent and dedicated faculty members who are expertise in the current technologies.
The department strives for uplifting the skill of the students to bring out innovative ideas from them with professionalism
and ethical values. The department offers BTech (accredited by NBA), MTech and PhD programmes. The department is a 
recognized research centre of APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, Thiruvananthapuram. The department 
has been maintaining an active interaction with the professional bodies like Computer Society of India and IEEE CS.
